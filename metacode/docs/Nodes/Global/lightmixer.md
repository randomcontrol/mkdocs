`Python: "lightmixer"`

When engaged, the lightmixer node detaches each light source into a separate channel. Then it provides controls to modulate each channel's color and power independently, without the need to re-render the whole image.

![Icon](lightmixer_swatch.png "Icon"){style="max-width: 80px;"}

## LightMixer

#### Engage LightMixer
`Python: "lightmixer_engage"`

Engages LightMixer to prepare the scene for realtime light editing. The engage action automatically assigns each light or lights group to a LightMixer channel.

#### Bypass LightMixer
`Python: "lightmixer_bypass"`

Bypasses the LightMixer controls to reveal the main render AOV.

#### Ambient channel alias
`Python: "lightmixer_ambient_alias"`

Identificative string for the ambient LightMixer channel.

#### Mute ambient
`Python: "lightmixer_ambient_mute"`

Mutes the ambient LightMixer channel.

#### Solo ambient
`Python: "lightmixer_ambient_solo"`

Leaves the ambient LightMixer channel active and mutes all other channels.

#### Ambient channel power
`Python: "lightmixer_ambient_power"`

Modulates the power of the ambient LightMixer channel.
